The intersection of the west

Welcome to Montana Connections Business Development Park. MCBDP is a collection of affordable business parcels located at the intersection of two major interstates, I-15 and I-90. Oh, and the BNSF and Union Pacific Class I Railway runs through the city. As an economic hub, businesses have no trouble operating from our business park.

How they grew

Montana Craft Malt

The newest tenant to the Montana Connections Business park is Montana Craft Malt. Breaking ground on a 10,000-ton state of the art craft malting facility, the company chose to locate in Montana Connections because of the strong logistical infrastructure, generous local financial incentives, and Montana Connection’s fantastic transportation links.
FedEx Freight

With local incentives from the Butte-Silver Bow local government, FedEx Freight built a 64-door cross dock facility in one of Montana Connections shovel ready parcels. The site has been so successful for FedEx Freight that work on an expansion to their existing facility will begin this summer.
REC Silicon

From their 245-acre parcel within Montana Connections, REC Silicon and their 200 full-time employees produce the world’s purest polysilicon and silicon gases for the global solar and electronics industries. REC, from their world headquarters in Oslo, Norway chose to locate one of their two North American operations in the Montana Connections Park because of the transportation links, cheap land availability, the Port of Montana’s Foreign Trade Zone (FTZ) and generous local incentives.

Why grow in Butte?

Gateway to the west

Within Montana Connections is the Port of Montana, a full service transload, warehouse and distribution facility. The Port is vital not only to many existing businesses within Montana Connections but to the wider region and can help any organization, large or small with their logistics needs.

Amazing Infrastructure

Montana Connections Industrial Park land can be purchased for $2,000 / acre sold in parcels as large as 250 acres or as small as 10. Currently, we have the ability to provide industrial water at 4 million GPD, wastewater at 2 million GPD, natural gas, three phase electrical power, potable water infrastructure and high-speed fiber coming soon, and extended rail service throughout the industrial park in the near future.

Driven and talented workforce

Southwest Montana is home to a highly skilled workforce, whether you need aerospace welders, experienced laborers, a mechanical engineer or ambitious truck drivers: southwest Montana has them all. Butte can also boast one of the world’s preeminent science and engineering colleges, Montana Tech, and a first-rate technical college in Highlands College.

Business friendly butte

Montana Connections Industrial Park is able to provide generous local tax incentives to business and organization interested in relocating or starting their businesses in Southwest Montana. The state of Montana offers numerous workforce, planning and infrastructure incentives that can be explored for any business to determine eligibility by Montana Connections team.
